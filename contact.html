Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the predictions?

Our AI model achieves 95% accuracy using advanced machine learning algorithms including XGBoost and collaborative filtering. The predictions are based on comprehensive user behavior analysis.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! We use enterprise-grade encryption and follow industry best practices to protect your data. Your privacy is our top priority.

How do price alerts work?

We continuously monitor prices across multiple platforms. When a price drop is detected on items matching your preferences, you'll receive an instant notification via email or SMS.

Can I integrate this with my e-commerce site?

Yes! We provide API integration options for e-commerce platforms. Contact our support team to learn more about enterprise solutions.

What categories are supported?

We currently support Electronics, Fashion, Home & Kitchen, Books, and Sports categories. More categories are being added regularly based on user demand.
